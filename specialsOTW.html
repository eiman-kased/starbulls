Specials Of The Week

Milk Chocolate Mondays
Come and enjoy hot milk chocolate for a party of 4 for only the price of 3. We'll serve a giant bowl of chocolate and only two spoons so sharing is caring! Milk Chocolate Mondays are a good way to get closer with family and friends.
Don't have friends?? Have no fear, matchmaking is here. Come to the counter and ask to sign up for a matchmaking table where we set you up with people also looking for new friends or family.... ($50/per table of 4)
Tingle Tuesdays
Have you had a long day of web development? Well come to StarBulls on Tuesdays and try our buffalo coffee and buffalo wings combo. I guarantee you will wake up so fast to code a whole project.
Bring your computer and pitch ideas to us and if we like it then we will give you one free half eating wing! If we really like the idea we'll throw in an extra bone in there. ($20/combo)
Wings n Wednesdays
Tis' the middle of an hardworking week, come to StarBulls and let us get you a great discount on wings. For $100 get yourself an all you can wing buffet for 5 people!!
We're talking about any flavor you can ask for; buffalo, BBQ, honey mustard, garlic, original, blueberry, watermelon, and many more. There's no other restaurant that does it the same way we do.
Now come and enjoy an authentic experience here in Buffalo. We will not dissapoint. ($100/per table of 4)
Tasty Thursdays
On Thursdays, we completely shut down our kitchen and open up our giant drinks bar. There will be discounted beverages all day long. Even though there aren't going to be any of our food, come and sit down with your friends and meet new people as well.
Use this day as a day to recharge yourself with a nice shot of siracha. Spicy not your style? Try our buy one get one of our BBQ beer pints. Live life by the edge and come to StarBulls on Tasty Thursdays. ($8.25)
Flying Fridays
If you come to StarBulls on a Friday, prepare to be taken on a ride. We completely transform the restaurant to make it simulate you're in the clouds. With a little imagination, you will be flying to your dream location. Since the environment incorporates a flying simulation, we figured the food should give you the same kind of experience. So come in and order a steak which we carved into a plane. Yes, that's right, for $80 get a place that is built by one giant ribeye along with 2 filet mignons, corn, mac&cheese, tomatoes, and a pickle. For $5 more add on a generous amount of ranch to create snow on the plane. ($80.00)
Seasoned Saturdays
Stop by on Seasoned Saturdays to try our special homemade dry rub wings seasoning. The seasoning is a secret recipe we keep in a glass bottle where no one will ever think to look. We are proud to be serving this kind of sauce to our supporters on Saturdays.
It's one of those sauces that you'd wanna order a seperate gallon of dry sauce just for your 6 pieces of wings. Along with the wings we serve the most seasoned fries you have ever eaten.
We are not done yet, adding onto the seasoned family, try our seasoned beer as well. ($20.95/per 12pc combo)
